The gym has a range of membership options to choose from, including:



Basic Membership - This membership gives you access to the gym's cardio and strength equipment, as well as the locker room facilities.
The cost for this membership is ksh 3000 per month.



Premium Membership - In addition to the basic features, this membership also gives you access to group fitness classes and personal training sessions.
The cost for this membership is ksh 5000 per month.



Family Membership - This membership is designed for families, and it allows up to four family members to use the gym facilities.
The cost for this membership is ksh 10000 per month.


FitLife Gym also offers discounts for students, seniors, and military personnel.
Additionally, they often run promotions and deals that can help you save money on your membership.
